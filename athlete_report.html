2024-2025 Elite Cyclists: Announcer Research Report

Comprehensive professional and personal highlights for 10 world-class athletes.
Prepared for broadcast and handout use.

Tadej Pogačar

1. Major Accomplishments

  • 2024: Won both the Giro d'Italia and Tour de France, first male since Pantani (1998) to achieve the double.
  • 2024: World Road Race Champion (Zurich, 100km solo attack).
  • 2024: Victories at Liège-Bastogne-Liège, Il Lombardia, Strade Bianche (81km solo), GP Montréal, Giro dell'Emilia, Volta a Catalunya.
  • 2023: Multiple stage wins and podiums in Grand Tours and Monuments.
  • UCI World No.1 for a record 152+ weeks.

2. Recent Professional Milestones (12 months)

  • Historic triple: Giro, Tour, and World Championships in one season.
  • 24+ victories in a single season, a feat not seen since the 1970s.
  • Led UAE Team Emirates to team victories and set new records for solo breakaways.

3. Social Media / Interesting Notes

  • Known for his playful, positive social media presence and engagement with fans.
  • Frequently posts behind-the-scenes training, team camaraderie, and personal moments with fiancée Urska Zigart (also a pro cyclist).
  • Active on Instagram and Twitter.

4. Quotes

"I just love to race my bike. Every day is a new adventure."
"Winning the Giro and Tour in one year is a dream come true. I owe it to my team and my family."
Wikipedia | ProCyclingStats

Remco Evenepoel

1. Major Accomplishments

  • 2024: Olympic double gold (road race and time trial, Paris) – first man ever to achieve this.
  • 2023: World Time Trial Champion, multiple Grand Tour stage wins.
  • Youngest man to win both world road and time trial titles.

2. Recent Professional Milestones (12 months)

  • Best young rider at his first Tour de France (2024).
  • Continued to rack up Grand Tour stage wins and major one-day victories.

3. Social Media / Interesting Notes

  • Active on Instagram and Twitter.
  • Known for sharing training insights, family moments, and motivational posts.

4. Quotes

"Winning Olympic gold in both events is something I never thought possible. It's a dream realized."
"I always race with my heart. That's what makes cycling beautiful."
Wikipedia | ProCyclingStats

Mads Pedersen

1. Major Accomplishments

  • 2024: Multiple stage wins in Grand Tours and Classics.
  • 2023: Consistent top-10 finishes in Monuments and WorldTour events.
  • 2019: World Road Race Champion.

2. Recent Professional Milestones (12 months)

  • Key victories in sprints and breakaways at the Tour de France and Vuelta a España.
  • Helped lead Lidl-Trek to team successes in Classics.

3. Social Media / Interesting Notes

  • Shares candid training and race-day moments on Instagram.
  • Known for his humor and approachable personality in the peloton.

4. Quotes

"You have to fight for every win. That's what makes it special."
"I love the Classics – the harder, the better."
Wikipedia | ProCyclingStats

Jonas Vingegaard

1. Major Accomplishments

  • Tour de France winner (2022, 2023), 2nd in 2024 after comeback from major injury.
  • Stage wins in Tour de France, Vuelta a España, Critérium du Dauphiné, Tirreno-Adriatico, Tour of Poland.

2. Recent Professional Milestones (12 months)

  • Recovered from a severe crash (7 broken ribs, punctured lungs) in spring 2024 to finish 2nd in the Tour de France and win a stage.
  • Birth of second child in summer 2024.
  • Set sights on a third Tour de France title and Vuelta a España in 2025.

3. Social Media / Interesting Notes

  • Known for his reserved, humble personality and family focus.
  • Shares training and team moments on Instagram.

4. Quotes

"This is of course a very emotional win for me. Coming back from a crash, it means a lot to me after all I went through in the last few months. Winning makes me think of that. I would have never been able to do this without my family."
"Now I believe that I can win the Tour de France."
Wikipedia | ProCyclingStats

Primož Roglič

1. Major Accomplishments

  • 2024: Record-equaling fourth Vuelta a España win.
  • 2023: Giro d'Italia overall victory.
  • Olympic Time Trial Champion (Tokyo 2020).
  • Multiple Grand Tour stage wins and Monuments.

2. Recent Professional Milestones (12 months)

  • Switched to Red Bull-Bora-hansgrohe in 2024.
  • Overcame Tour de France crash to win Vuelta a España.
  • Continues to be a top GC contender in all Grand Tours.

3. Social Media / Interesting Notes

  • Shares motivational posts and family moments on Instagram and Twitter.
  • Known for his resilience and positive attitude after setbacks.

4. Quotes

"Hopefully this year, we won't repeat the mistakes and we can react better if we find ourselves in the same situation."
"In his own mind, the Tour de France is still the main goal. It's the only race he hasn't won yet."
Wikipedia | ProCyclingStats

Wout van Aert

1. Major Accomplishments

  • 2024: Multiple stage wins at Vuelta a España, Kuurne-Brussel-Kuurne victory.
  • 2023: Consistent podiums in Monuments and Grand Tours.
  • 2020: Milan-San Remo Monument victory.

2. Recent Professional Milestones (12 months)

  • Recovered from two major crashes in 2024, returned to top form at Vuelta a España.
  • Bronze medal in time trial at Paris Olympics.
  • Set to focus on Giro d'Italia, Tour de France, and spring classics in 2025.

3. Social Media / Interesting Notes

  • Massive social media following; shares recovery, family, and training updates on Instagram.
  • Known for his versatility and rivalry with Mathieu van der Poel.

4. Quotes

"The fact that I was at top level again in the Vuelta gives a lot of confidence."
"The Tour of Flanders and Paris-Roubaix are once again circled red in the agenda."
Wikipedia | ProCyclingStats

João Almeida

1. Major Accomplishments

  • 2024: Stage wins at Tour de Suisse, Paris-Nice, Itzulia Basque Country.

  • Multiple GC podiums in WorldTour stage races.

2. Recent Professional Milestones (12 months)

  • GC win at Tour de Romandie (2025), multiple stage wins in 2024-25.
  • Continued progression as a top GC contender for UAE Team Emirates.

3. Social Media / Interesting Notes

  • Active on Instagram.
  • Known for his calm, analytical approach and strong fan support in Portugal.

4. Quotes

"Estou perto do topo e posso chegar onde quero." ("I'm close to the top and I can get wherever I want.")
"Regularity is one of my advantages in comparison with my opponents. That comes from genetics, but also hard work."

Mathieu van der Poel

1. Major Accomplishments

  • 2024: Tour of Flanders and Paris-Roubaix double, 2023 Road World Champion.
  • Six-time Cyclo-Cross World Champion, 2024 Gravel World Champion.
  • Multiple Monument wins, including Milan-San Remo (2023).

2. Recent Professional Milestones (12 months)

  • Dominant performances in Classics and World Championships.
  • Continued rivalry with Wout van Aert, inspiring fans worldwide.

3. Social Media / Interesting Notes

  • Shares training, racing, and family moments on Instagram and Strava.
  • Comes from a legendary cycling family (son of Adrie, grandson of Raymond Poulidor).

4. Quotes

"Yesterday I didn't have the best day again, which is a shame because I would have liked to help Jasper [Philipsen] in the sprint preparation. I suffered quite a bit. I was also behind a crash, but I don't know if I could have done much for the rest. It's a shame, we have limited opportunities and I would have liked to have done a good lead-out for Jasper again. Hopefully today."
"Do I want to be the first to win the Tour of Flanders four times? It's kind of like with the cyclo-cross world championships. I focus more on the race itself than on records."
Wikipedia | ProCyclingStats

Kristen Faulkner

1. Major Accomplishments

  • 2024: Double Olympic gold (road race and team pursuit, Paris) – first U.S. woman to win both at a single Games.
  • 2024: USA National Road Race Champion.
  • 2023: Pan American Time Trial Champion.

2. Recent Professional Milestones (12 months)

  • Overcame a serious training crash in 2023 to return to top form.
  • Major media attention and community recognition in Alaska and the U.S.
  • Continues to inspire as a latecomer to pro cycling (Harvard grad, ex-VC).

3. Social Media / Interesting Notes

  • Shares her journey from finance to cycling on Instagram and Facebook.
  • Known for her advocacy for women in sports and tech, and for motivational speaking.

4. Quotes

"I'm still pinching myself. I think I'll wake up in a week, and it still won't feel real. It's going to take a long time for this to sink in."
"I took a really big risk a few years ago to come to pursue my dream. I made it happen."
Wikipedia | ProCyclingStats

Brodie Chapman

1. Major Accomplishments

  • 2024: Rainbow jersey-winning ride in Mixed Relay TTT at UCI Road World Championships (Zurich).
  • 2023: Australian National Road Race Champion.
  • 2025: Australian National Time Trial Champion.

2. Recent Professional Milestones (12 months)

  • First Australian to sign for UAE Team ADQ WorldTour team (2025).
  • Consistent podiums in time trials and strong performances in European stage races.

3. Social Media / Interesting Notes

  • Shares her cycling journey and adventures on Instagram.
  • Known for her resilience, positive attitude, and support for women's cycling.

4. Quotes

"Each year, my goal's always just to improve. I have to remind myself I've got a whole year of experience behind me. My team from the get-go put absolutely everything into dialling in my position and equipment. That made the biggest difference, I think, for me this year, was having a really great position and equipment from Team UAE ADQ."
"When you want to improve and do see improvements, it definitely feels really good, but the sights are always set higher and higher."
Wikipedia | ProCyclingStats
